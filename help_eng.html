How to Play ShiftyWords

Objective: Guess the daily word by rearranging the columns of letters. Play a a non-English version and get a mini language lesson after every game! Switch langauges using the globe in the footer.

How to Play:

  1. Select Letters: Tap on a letter from any column to fill a box on the guessing board. Note that the letters don't have to form an actual word. You can submit any arrangement as your guess.
  2. Submit Your Guess: When all boxes are filled, press the "Check" button to submit your guess.
  3. Move Columns: Use the arrows above or below each column to rearrange them until they form your final answer. The leftmost and rightmost arrows wrap around, so you can cycle through the columns. Remember, you have a limited number of moves!
  4. Feedback: The tiles will change colors:
    • Green: Correct letter in the right spot.
    • Yellow: Letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
    • Gray: Letter is not in the word.
  5. Winning: If your guess is correct, the row will turn purple, and you'll win the game.
  6. Get a free hint: The logo is hiding one. Look carefully and you'll find a small dot when a hint is waiting. One reveal per day, per language. The puzzle will whisper if you've been struggling for a while.
  7. Moves & Board Size:
    • You start with 45 moves. With each consecutive win in your win streak, you begin with 7 fewer moves—but you'll always have a minimum of 21 moves.
    • Additionally, your win streak reduces the number of guessing rows (making the game more challenging):
      • Win streak less than 3: 5 rows (Gray Level)
      • Win streak between 3 and 5: 4 rows (Purple Level)
      • Win streak between 6 and 9: 3 rows (Gold Level)
      • Win streak 10 or above: 2 rows (Impossible Level)

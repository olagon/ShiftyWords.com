Privacy Policy

Be kind.

That includes how we treat your information. We collect as little as we can. We never sell what we have. We only use it to make the puzzle better. The rest of this page is just the detail behind that promise.

What we collect

ShiftyWords does not ask you to sign in or share an email. There is no account.

Your streak, win streak, and daily game state live in your own browser through cookies and localStorage. You can clear them at any time using your browser's site data tools.

The site uses Google Analytics to understand how the puzzle is used. This is set up to be as private as we know how to make it.

What the analytics actually record

Beyond simple page visits, we record anonymous gameplay events so we can see which parts of the puzzle are working and where to invest our time. These include things like game start, win, loss, current language, and the level reached.

None of these events include your name, your email, your IP address, or anything that identifies you. They are counts and aggregates, not a record of any individual person's play.

The reason we record this is to make the puzzle better. If we see that most players never make it past the Gray Level, that tells us we need to balance the difficulty. We are not trying to build a profile of you. We are trying to build a better game.

What we do not do

We do not sell any data. Ever.

We do not run ads.

We do not track you around the web.

We do not share your progress with anyone.

How your data is stored

Local progress lives in your browser via cookies and localStorage. Nothing is stored on a server because there is no server-side account.

If you want your data deleted

Clear your browser's site data for shiftywords.com. That removes everything we know about your play.

Contact

For privacy questions, feedback, or anything else, send a message on LinkedIn.

Changes to this policy

If anything here changes, we will update this page and note the change at the bottom.

Terms of Use

Be kind.

That is the heart of how this puzzle should be used. Be kind to other players. Be kind to the languages you play in and the people who carry them. Be kind to the makers and translators who put their care into building this. Everything else on this page is just the detail.

Using ShiftyWords

The puzzle is free to play. You do not need to sign up, sign in, or pay anything. Your streak and progress live in your own browser.

The code is open source under the MIT License. You are welcome to read it, learn from it, fork it, and build on it.

What we ask of you

Treat other players with kindness. Do not abuse the service or try to break it for others. Do not scrape the dictionaries to build a competing product without credit.

Treat the languages with kindness. Each language in this puzzle belongs to a living community. Play it as a guest.

Accuracy of the words

We do our best to make every word valid in its language, with proper diacritics where they belong. Languages are alive and dictionaries disagree, so you may see entries you would spell or accept differently. If you spot an error, please let us know.

No warranty

The puzzle is provided "as is." We do our best, but we cannot guarantee it will always work, that your streak will never reset, or that every word is perfect. Use it for fun, and take it lightly.

If we ever shut it down

If for any reason this site goes away, we will give notice on the site. The code is open source, so the puzzle itself can outlive any single hosting choice.

Changes to these terms

If anything here changes, we will update this page and note the change at the bottom.

Questions or feedback

Send a message on LinkedIn for anything. Word corrections, feature ideas, bug reports, or just to say hello are all welcome.
